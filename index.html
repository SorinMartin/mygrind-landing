FAQ

Quick answers for testers and early users.

Is MyGrind a gambling or betting app?

No. MyGrind does not provide gambling, betting, or real-money games. It only tracks and analyzes sessions played elsewhere.

Does it support multiple currencies?

Yes. Record sessions in USD/EUR/RON and view unified results using your selected base currency.

What are challenges?

Challenges are structured goals (hours, profit, volume, streaks) you can track over time, optionally with leaderboards for friends.

How do I join the beta?

Install TestFlight on iPhone and use the invite link.
